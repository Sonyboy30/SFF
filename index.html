Welcome to Our Site

Welcome to the Soni Family Foundation. We’re on a mission to unlock opportunity through education. Based in Dallas, SFF partners with schools and nonprofits to deliver microgrants, mentorship, and learning experiences for students who need them most.

Unlocking Brighter Futures

At the Soni Family Foundation, we believe every child has the ability to become a genius. We’re investing in under-resourced schools and nonprofits in South and West Dallas by funding after-school programs, mentorship, and hands-on learning experiences.
