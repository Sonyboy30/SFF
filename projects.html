1) Challenging the Norms of Learning

Education shouldn’t be a privilege. We fund and co-design programs that make learning stick—mentorship, accessible spaces, and evidence-driven teaching that turn curiosity into capability.

2) Redefining What It Means to Be Well

Wellness starts with safety, belonging, and steady routines. We support initiatives that build emotional resilience, mental clarity, and a sense of being cared for—so learning can thrive.

3) Rethinking How Progress Is Made

Our R&D is community-based: listen, prototype, measure, iterate. We pilot new tutoring formats and classroom supports, then scale what the data—and students—say works.
