Our Story

The Soni Family Foundation is a Dallas-based nonprofit focused on expanding educational opportunity through thoughtful, measurable action.

We collaborate with Title I public schools and local nonprofits to support learning, wellness, and pilot programs that strengthen outcomes for students and families.

In 2025, SFF received official 501(c)(3) status, enabling us to expand our work and make contributions tax-deductible (EIN: 33-3328044).

Our mission is simple: listen closely, support what works, and scale solutions that create lasting change.

How We Work

Community-Informed

We start by listening to educators, families, and students before taking action.

Evidence-Based

We prioritize practical solutions and track outcomes to refine our approach.

Built to Scale

We pilot, measure, and expand initiatives that demonstrate real impact.
