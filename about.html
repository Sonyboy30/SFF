Our Story

The Soni Family Foundation (SFF) is a Dallas-based nonprofit committed to closing opportunity gaps in education — one school, one program, one student at a time.

Founded by the Soni family with deep roots in service and equity, SFF partners directly with Title I public schools and local nonprofits in South and West Dallas to deliver impactful microgrants, after-school programs, and mentorship opportunities that improve educational outcomes for underserved students.

We believe that the future of Dallas depends on how we support the kids who need it most today. We envision a Dallas where zip code doesn’t determine opportunity, and every student has access to the tools, relationships, and experiences that unlock their full potential.
