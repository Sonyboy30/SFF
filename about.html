Our Story

The Soni Family Foundation (SFF) is a Dallas-based nonprofit committed to expanding educational opportunity through community-led work and measurable impact.

We collaborate with Title I public schools and local nonprofits across Dallas to support learning, wellness, and pilot programs that strengthen outcomes for students and families.

In 2025, SFF received official 501(c)(3) status, enabling us to expand our work and make contributions tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law (EIN: 33-3328044).

Our goal is simple: support what works, listen closely to the community, and scale solutions that help more young people succeed.

How we work

Community-informed

We start by listening to students, educators, and partner organizations before we fund or build anything.

Evidence-based

We prioritize practical approaches and track results so we can improve programs over time.

Built to scale

We pilot, refine, and expand initiatives that demonstrate real impact in the communities we serve.
